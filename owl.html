Welcome to the CSU-Pueblo Online Writing Lab (OWL) submission corner!

The OWL is a service of the CSU-Pueblo Writing Room, located in LARC 251. Our OWL writing consultants can provide you with feedback on any kind of writing that you are doing or questions you have about writing. The CSU-Pueblo OWL is open to both the public and CSU-Pueblo students. You do not need to be a CSU-Pueblo student to receive online feedback. Anybody can submit!

OWL guidelines:

  1. It can take up to two business days to receive your online session via e-mail, so please submit well in advance of deadlines.
  2. Papers submitted after 5pm, on weekends, or when the university is closed will be reviewed the next available weekday.
  3. Remember, we do not write papers, we do not proofread for the purpose of correcting all errors (we leave that to the writer), and we do not discuss a paper's potential grade. Instead, your tutor will help you build upon your writing and revision skills for current and future writing!
  4. Sending complete and accurate information is required when using the Online Writing Lab. Writing might not be returned if necessary and accurate information is not provided.
  5. If you have any questions about the OWL service or did not receive a response after two business days (not including days the university is closed), please contact us. We are here to help!
